# CalcuSnapVault FAQ

## How do I open the vault?

Enter your PIN on the calculator screen and tap `=`.

## What is the default PIN?

The default PIN is `1234` unless you changed it in Settings.

## Why did the app lock when I put my phone down?

`CalcuSnapVault` can auto-lock when the proximity sensor is covered. You can manage that in `Settings`.

## How do I restore Premium?

Open `Settings` and tap `Restore Purchases`.

## Where are security breach photos managed?

Open `Settings` and go to `Security Breach Photos`.
