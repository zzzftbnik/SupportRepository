# LunoVault Support Guide

If you need help with `LunoVault`, use this page.

## Contact Support

Email `zzzftbnik@icloud.com` and include:

- Your device model
- Your iOS version
- A short description of the issue

## Unlocking the Vault

1. Open `LunoVault`.
2. Enter your PIN on the calculator screen.
3. Tap `=`.
4. If Face ID is enabled, you can also unlock with biometrics.

## Restoring Premium

1. Open the app.
2. Go to `Settings`.
3. Tap `Restore Purchases`.
4. Wait for the restore to complete.

## Security Breach Photos

1. Unlock the vault.
2. Swipe down inside the vault.
3. Open `Security Breach`.
4. View or clear saved breach photos there.

## Resetting the Vault

1. Open `Settings`.
2. Scroll to `Reset`.
3. Tap `Reset Vault`.
4. Confirm the action.

Resetting the vault permanently removes stored vault content from the device.
