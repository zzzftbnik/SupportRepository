# LunoVault Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

## Overview

`LunoVault` is built to help you privately store photos, videos, and files behind a calculator-style interface. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how that information is handled, and what choices you have.

## At a Glance

- Your vault content is stored on your device
- The app may use Firebase authentication for app services
- Premium purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit
- Camera access is only used for the optional security breach photo feature
- We do not sell your personal information

## Information Stored on Your Device

`LunoVault` may store the following locally on your device so the app can work properly:

- Photos and videos you import into the vault
- Files you choose to store in the vault
- Album names and organization data
- App lock settings such as PIN and appearance preferences
- Optional security breach photos captured after failed unlock attempts

## Authentication and App Services

The app may use Firebase authentication to support sign-in, account state, and related services. Your vault PIN is managed locally on your device.

## Purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card information.

## Permissions

`LunoVault` may request access to:

- Your photo library, to import media into the vault or save media back to your library
- Your camera, only for the optional security breach photo feature
- Face ID, if you choose to enable biometric unlock

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information is only shared with service providers or platform services when needed to operate the app, such as Apple or Firebase.

## Your Controls

You can choose to:

- Disable Face ID
- Disable the security breach photo feature
- Clear stored breach photos
- Restore purchases
- Reset the vault
- Delete your account

## Data Deletion

You can delete vault content by removing individual items or by using the `Reset Vault` option inside the app settings. If account deletion is available in the app, that action clears supported account data associated with the app experience.
