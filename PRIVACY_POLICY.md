# CalcuSnapVault Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

## Overview

`CalcuSnapVault` is built to protect private media behind a calculator-style interface. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

## Information You Store

The app may store the following on your device:

- Photos
- Videos
- Files
- Album names
- Lock preferences
- Security breach photos captured after failed unlock attempts

## Authentication

`CalcuSnapVault` may use anonymous Firebase authentication to support app state and future account-linked features. Your vault PIN is managed locally on the device.

## Purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. `CalcuSnapVault` does not receive your full payment card details.

## Camera and Photo Library Access

- Photo library access is used to import media into the vault and optionally save media back to your library.
- Camera access is used only for the optional security breach photo feature.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only with platform and service providers when necessary to operate the app, such as Apple or Firebase.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Disable Face ID
- Disable security breach photo capture
- Clear breach photos
- Restore purchases
- Reset the vault
